What is JM Movies?

JM_Movies is a subscription-based streaming service that allows our members to watch TV shows and movies without commercials on an internet-connected device.   

You can also download TV shows and movies to your iOS, Android, or Windows 10 device and watch without an internet connection.

If you're already a member and would like to learn more about using Netflix, visit Getting started with JM_Movies.

TV Shows & Movies

JM_Movies content varies by region and may change over time. You can watch from a wide variety of award-winning JM_Movies Originals, TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more. 

The more you watch, the better JM_Movies gets at recommending TV shows and movies we think you’ll enjoy.

Supported Devices

You can watch JM_Movies through any internet-connected device that offers the JM_Movies app, including smart TVs, game consoles, streaming media players, set-top boxes, smartphones, and tablets. You can also watch JM_Movies on your computer using an internet browser.

Note:
The JM_Movies app may come pre-loaded on certain devices, or you may need to download the JM_Movies app onto your device. JM_Movies app functionality may differ between devices.

Plans and Pricing

Each JM_Movies plan determines the number of devices you can watch JM_Movies on at the same time and if you prefer to view in Standard Definition (SD), High Definition (HD), or Ultra High Definition (UHD).

Get Started!

Follow these easy steps to start watching Netflix today:

  1. Visit JM_Movies.com/signup.

  2. Choose the plan that’s right for you.

  3. Create an account by entering your email address and creating a password.

  4. Enter a payment method. As a Netflix member, you are charged once a month on the date you signed up. 

That's it. Enjoy Netflix!
